ABOUT JGS MALLAPUR (ICSE/ ISC/ IBDP) In the year 2011 we reached a new milestone where the student head count touched to around 5000. Hence we branched out to a new campus at Mallapur. Classes VIII to X were shifted from Habsiguda to this branch. This campus is well equipped with all amenities like large class rooms, fully equipped science labs, spacious libraries with a wide collection of books, ATAL lab, audio visual room, language lab, computer labs, a dining hall, sate of the art gym and many more. The spacious ground is used as a playground for various sports such as cricket, basket ball, skating and for other games and activities. In the year 2017 the school extended to a new block at Mallapur for classes LKG to VII.Now the campus has two blocks, the Senior Wing and Junior Wing. Now the Junior Wing hosts classes KG to VIII. And the Senior Wing for Classes IX , X, ISC and IBDP Grade XI & XII.
